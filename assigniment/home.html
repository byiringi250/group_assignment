Welcome to Ecole primary Regina Pacis

Ecole primary Regina Pacis,
we are dedicated to creating an inspiring and joyful learning environment for children. Our progressive approach encourages curiosity, exploration, creativity, and play, making education a delightful adventure. Through our unique Field Education Program, we extend learning beyond the classroom, connecting it to the broader world and nurturing a sense of community and global awareness. We take pride in our diverse community, where every child is valued, and social, emotional, and academic growth are given equal importance. Our mission is to lay a robust foundation for our students' future schooling and for a life filled with purpose and value. Capitol Hill Day School graduates emerge as

confident, compassionate young individuals, equipped with the skills to navigate their communities, their educational journeys, and their lives with grace and confidence. Our faculty members are not only passionate educators but also experts in their fields and the developmental stages they teach. They form a supportive alliance with students, fostering a collaborative spirit and mutual respect. At Capitol Hill Day School, teachers and students together celebrate each other’s unique strengths, creating a vibrant and inclusive learning community.

At Ecole primary Regina Pacis, we believe that children thrive when they are actively engaged and when concepts are presented in meaningful and creative ways. Our exceptional Field Education program, with over 300 field trips annually, bridges the classroom with the real world, instilling problem-solving and critical thinking skills in our students. Our small class sizes and project-based teaching approach
foster a lifelong love of learning, where knowledge is gained through hands-on, experiential methods rather than rote memorization and testing. Our collaborative and inquiry-based curriculum builds on students' natural curiosity, encouraging them to make connections across disciplines and subject areas. At Capitol Hill Day School, students learn to respect the world around them, and to care for their
community and one another. Our graduates emerge as confident, academically prepared, and socially adept individuals, excelling in a wide range of high schools. They continue to embody our school’s motto: “Explore, Engage, and Excel.”
